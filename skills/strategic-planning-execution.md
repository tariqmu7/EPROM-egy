# Skill — Strategic Planning & Execution

> التخطيط الاستراتيجي والتنفيذ

| Field | Value |
|---|---|

| **Assessment method** | INTERVIEW |
| **Applies to** | General-department and senior management roles |

## Description

Translating company direction into clear objectives, budgets and measurable targets for a general department, then driving disciplined execution and course-correction against them.

## Proficiency Levels

| Level | Label | What it means for this skill |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Awareness | Understands the department objectives and how own work supports them |
| 2 | Basic | Contributes to plans and tracks progress against set targets with guidance |
| 3 | Intermediate | Builds objectives, budgets and plans for a unit and monitors delivery |
| 4 | Advanced | Sets multi-unit strategy and KPIs; aligns resources and adjusts to performance |
| 5 | Expert | Owns the general-department strategy and execution system; aligns leadership behind it |
