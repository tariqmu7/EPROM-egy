# Skill — Project Contract Follow-up & Claims Management

> متابعة عقود المشروعات وإدارة المطالبات

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Category** | Business / Commercial |
| **Assessment method** | WORK_RECORD_REVIEW |

## Description

Following up project contracts through execution — administering variations, progress and payment certificates, and managing claims and disputes (e.g. under FIDIC-style conditions of contract) to protect the company's entitlement and commercial value.

## Proficiency Levels

| Level | Label | What it means for this skill |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Awareness | Understands the project-contract lifecycle and what a variation / claim is |
| 2 | Basic | Tracks contractual correspondence, milestones and payment applications with guidance |
| 3 | Intermediate | Administers variations and prepares routine claims and contractual notices independently |
| 4 | Advanced | Leads claims strategy and dispute resolution on major contracts; protects entitlement and value |
| 5 | Expert | Sets the claims / dispute and project-contract governance approach across all projects |

**Behaviourally-anchored criteria** (rated 1–5, weighted to 100):

- Tracks contract obligations and milestones and flags deviations early. — **50%**
- Prepares and manages variations / claims with proper substantiation. — **50%**

## Notes

The "follow-up" half of the department's mandate. Closely tied to Contract Drafting & Administration and Commercial & Financial Acumen. Evidence: variation orders, claim files, payment certificates, dispute records.

## References / Standards

- FIDIC — Conditions of Contract (Red / Yellow / Silver Book): variations, claims and dispute procedures.
- RICS — *Conflict Avoidance, Management & Dispute Resolution* and quantum / claims guidance.
- PMI *PMBOK Guide* — Project Procurement Management: control procurements (administration & claims).
