# Skill — Marketing Program Management

> إدارة برامج التسويق

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Category** | Business / Commercial |
| **Assessment method** | WORK_RECORD_REVIEW |
| **Applies to** | Business Development & External Contracting; SP and above |

## Description

Planning, running, and measuring marketing campaigns and brand initiatives that generate qualified leads and support the company's commercial positioning.

## Proficiency Levels

| Level | Label | What it means for this skill |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Awareness | Knows the company's marketing channels and brand basics |
| 2 | Basic | Executes defined campaign tasks and tracks simple metrics |
| 3 | Intermediate | Plans and delivers campaigns end‑to‑end with a budget and KPIs |
| 4 | Advanced | Designs multi‑channel programs tied to pipeline goals; optimises on ROI |
| 5 | Expert | Owns the marketing program portfolio and sets measurement standards |

## Assessment Methods

Configured inline on the skill (**Admin › Skills › Competency Standard › Assessment Methods**) and conforms to **ISO 10667, ISO/IEC 17024, OPITO**.

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Method | Work Record / Case Study Review |
| Evidence validity | 12 months |
| Min. approved records | 2 |
| Frequency | Any time within the year (annual) |
| Audience | All employees assigned the skill |

**Prompt** — Review submitted campaign plans, budgets and KPI / ROI reports as evidence of competence.

**Behaviourally-anchored criteria** (rated 1–5, weighted to 100):

- Plans and delivers campaigns end-to-end with a budget and KPIs. — **50%**
- Measures and optimises on ROI / lead generation. — **50%**

## Notes

Evidence: campaign plans, performance dashboards, lead‑generation results.
