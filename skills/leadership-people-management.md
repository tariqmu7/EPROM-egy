# Skill — Leadership & People Management

> القيادة وإدارة الأفراد

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Category** | Management |
| **Assessment method** | THREE_SIXTY_EVALUATION |
| **Applies to** | Managers — section, department and general-department leadership roles |

## Description

Leading managers and teams across multiple units — setting direction, developing capability, managing performance, and building a high-performing, accountable commercial organisation.

## Proficiency Levels

| Level | Label | What it means for this skill |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Awareness | Understands team goals and supports colleagues constructively |
| 2 | Basic | Guides a small task or team and gives basic feedback with support |
| 3 | Intermediate | Leads a team: sets goals, manages performance and develops members |
| 4 | Advanced | Leads managers across units; builds capability and a strong performance culture |
| 5 | Expert | Shapes leadership and talent strategy for the general department; develops other leaders |

## Assessment Methods

Configured inline on the skill (**Admin › Skills › Competency Standard › Assessment Methods**) and conforms to **360° Multi-Rater, BARS, SHRM BASK**.

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Method | 360° Multi-Rater Evaluation |
| Rater weights | Self 10% / Peer 30% / Manager 60% |
| Assessor (manager rater) | Department Manager |
| Frequency | Any time within the year (annual) |
| Audience | All employees assigned the skill |

**Behaviourally-anchored criteria** (rated 1–5, weighted to 100):

- Sets clear direction and goals for teams / managers across units. — **35%**
- Develops capability and manages performance fairly. — **35%**
- Builds an accountable, high-performing culture. — **30%**

## Notes

Leadership competency assessed via 360° multi-rater feedback; the manager-rater is the post-holder’s own line manager.
