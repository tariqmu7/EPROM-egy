## Description

Understanding the oil‑and‑gas value chain, key players, regulatory environment and competitive landscape (Egypt and regional) to ground commercial decisions in sector reality.

## Proficiency Levels

| Level | Label | What it means for this skill |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Awareness | Knows the basic upstream/midstream/downstream value chain |
| 2 | Basic | Familiar with key local operators, services and terminology |
| 3 | Intermediate | Understands market structure, regulation and competitor positioning |
| 4 | Advanced | Reads sector dynamics and links them to company opportunities and risks |
| 5 | Expert | Recognised sector authority; anticipates market and regulatory shifts |
