# Skill — Digital Marketing & Brand Management

> التسويق الرقمي وإدارة العلامة التجارية

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Category** | Business / Commercial |
| **Assessment method** | WORK_RECORD_REVIEW |
| **Applies to** | Business Development & External Contracting; SP and above |

## Description

Using digital channels, content and brand positioning to raise the company's profile and generate demand in target segments.

## Proficiency Levels

| Level | Label | What it means for this skill |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Awareness | Understands the company's brand and main digital channels |
| 2 | Basic | Publishes content and updates with guidance |
| 3 | Intermediate | Runs digital campaigns and manages brand consistency |
| 4 | Advanced | Plans channel/content strategy tied to demand‑generation goals |
| 5 | Expert | Owns brand and digital strategy; sets standards and measurement |

## Assessment Methods

Configured inline on the skill (**Admin › Skills › Competency Standard › Assessment Methods**) and conforms to **ISO 10667, ISO/IEC 17024, OPITO**.

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Method | Work Record / Case Study Review |
| Evidence validity | 12 months |
| Min. approved records | 2 |
| Frequency | Any time within the year (annual) |
| Audience | All employees assigned the skill |

**Prompt** — Review digital campaigns, content and brand assets produced as evidence.

**Behaviourally-anchored criteria** (rated 1–5, weighted to 100):

- Runs digital campaigns and maintains brand consistency across channels. — **50%**
- Ties channel / content activity to demand-generation goals and measures it. — **50%**

## Notes

Complements Marketing Program Management. Evidence: campaigns, content, channel analytics.
