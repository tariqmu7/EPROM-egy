# Skill — Business Development Strategy

> استراتيجية تنمية الأعمال

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Category** | Business / Commercial |
| **Assessment method** | INTERVIEW |
| **Applies to** | Business Development & External Contracting; SP and above |

## Description

Evaluating and developing growth strategy aligned to company objectives and services — translating market intelligence into a prioritised pipeline of qualified opportunities.

## Proficiency Levels

| Level | Label | What it means for this skill |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Awareness | Understands the company's services and growth objectives |
| 2 | Basic | Contributes ideas to opportunity lists under direction |
| 3 | Intermediate | Builds and maintains an opportunity pipeline; applies qualification criteria |
| 4 | Advanced | Develops segment/market entry strategies and business cases that win new work |
| 5 | Expert | Sets the BD strategy and growth roadmap; aligns leadership behind it |

**Behaviourally-anchored criteria** (rated 1–5, weighted to 100):

- Translates market intelligence into a qualified, prioritised opportunity pipeline. — **40%**
