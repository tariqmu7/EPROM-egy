# Skill — Contractual Risk & Compliance Management

> إدارة المخاطر التعاقدية والالتزام

| Field | Value |

## Description

Identifying, allocating and mitigating contractual and commercial risk, and ensuring contracts comply with company policy, delegated authority, governance and applicable law and regulation.

## Proficiency Levels

| Level | Label | What it means for this skill |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Awareness | Aware of common contract risks and the company's approval / authority limits |
| 2 | Basic | Flags risk items and checks contracts against a compliance checklist with guidance |
| 3 | Intermediate | Assesses and allocates contractual risk and ensures compliance independently |
| 4 | Advanced | Shapes risk-allocation and mitigation strategy on complex / high-value contracts |
| 5 | Expert | Owns the contractual risk and compliance framework; advises leadership and assures governance |

**Behaviourally-anchored criteria** (rated 1–5, weighted to 100):

- Identifies and quantifies contractual risk and compliance exposure. — **40%**

## Notes

The control layer over drafting, negotiation and claims. Evidence: risk registers, compliance reviews, deviation / approval records.
