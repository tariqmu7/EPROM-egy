## Description

Applying pricing, margin, ROI and business‑case logic to evaluate opportunities and make commercially sound decisions.

## Proficiency Levels

| Level | Label | What it means for this skill |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Awareness | Understands basic cost, price and margin concepts |
| 2 | Basic | Reads financial figures and builds simple cost estimates with guidance |
| 3 | Intermediate | Builds business cases and pricing models for individual opportunities |
| 4 | Advanced | Evaluates deal economics and risk; advises on pricing and margin trade‑offs |
| 5 | Expert | Sets commercial/pricing policy and benchmarks across the portfolio |
