# Skill — Contract Drafting & Administration

> صياغة وإدارة العقود

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Category** | Business / Commercial |
| **Assessment method** | WORK_RECORD_REVIEW |
| **Applies to** | Business Development & External Contracting (External Contracts/Bids); SP and above |

## Description

Drafting, issuing and administering contracts and their amendments — building clear, enforceable terms, maintaining the contract record, and ensuring obligations, milestones and approvals are tracked through the full contract lifecycle.

## Proficiency Levels

| Level | Label | What it means for this skill |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Awareness | Understands standard contract structure and key clause types |
| 2 | Basic | Prepares contracts from approved templates and maintains the contract register with guidance |
| 3 | Intermediate | Drafts and administers complete contracts and variations independently |
| 4 | Advanced | Tailors complex contract terms and leads administration across a portfolio of contracts |
| 5 | Expert | Sets contract templates, clause libraries and administration standards for the company |

## Assessment Methods

Configured inline on the skill (**Admin › Skills › Competency Standard › Assessment Methods**) and conforms to **ISO 10667, ISO/IEC 17024, OPITO**.

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|

| Evidence validity | 12 months |

**Prompt** — Review drafted / administered contracts and amendment records as evidence.

**Behaviourally-anchored criteria** (rated 1–5, weighted to 100):

- Drafts and administers contracts accurately and compliantly. — **50%**
- Maintains complete records, variations and obligations tracking. — **50%**

## Notes

The core craft skill of the External Contracts department. Tied to Contract Negotiation and Contractual Risk & Compliance. Evidence: drafted contracts, amendments, contract registers.

## References / Standards

- World Commerce & Contracting (WorldCC / IACCM) — *Contract & Commercial Management* practitioner competencies (drafting, administration, lifecycle management).
- FIDIC — standard forms of contract (oil & gas / EPC) as the drafting and administration baseline.
