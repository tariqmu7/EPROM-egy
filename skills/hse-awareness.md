## Description

Baseline understanding of Health, Safety and Environment principles and the company's HSE policy — the safety culture expected of every employee in an oil‑and‑gas organisation.

## Proficiency Levels

| Level | Label | What it means for this skill |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Awareness | Knows core HSE rules, hazards and reporting obligations |
| 2 | Basic | Applies HSE procedures correctly in own work and worksites |
| 3 | Intermediate | Identifies risks proactively and promotes safe behavior in the team |
| 4 | Advanced | Embeds HSE into planning and decisions; coaches others |
| 5 | Expert | Shapes HSE culture and policy across the organisation |
