# Skill — Proposal & Technical Writing

> كتابة العروض والمقترحات الفنية

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Category** | Business / Commercial |
| **Assessment method** | WORK_RECORD_REVIEW |
| **Applies to** | Business Development & External Contracting; SP and above |

## Description

Producing clear, compliant and persuasive proposals, tenders and commercial bids that communicate value and meet submission requirements.

## Proficiency Levels

| Level | Label | What it means for this skill |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Awareness | Understands proposal structure and compliance basics |
| 2 | Basic | Drafts sections from templates with review |
| 3 | Intermediate | Writes complete, compliant proposals independently |
| 4 | Advanced | Crafts win‑themed, client‑focused proposals that lift win rate |
| 5 | Expert | Sets proposal standards and templates; coaches writers |

## Assessment Methods

Configured inline on the skill (**Admin › Skills › Competency Standard › Assessment Methods**) and conforms to **ISO 10667, ISO/IEC 17024, OPITO**.

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Method | Work Record / Case Study Review |
| Evidence validity | 12 months |
| Min. approved records | 2 |
| Frequency | Any time within the year (annual) |
| Audience | All employees assigned the skill |

**Prompt** — Review authored proposals / tenders for clarity, compliance and persuasiveness.

**Behaviourally-anchored criteria** (rated 1–5, weighted to 100):

- Produces complete, compliant proposals that meet submission requirements. — **50%**
- Writes clear, client-focused, win-themed content. — **50%**

## Notes

Supports Bid & Tender Management. Evidence: submitted proposals and bid documents.
