# Skill — Bid & Tender Management (RFQ / RFP / ITT)

> إدارة العطاءات والمناقصات

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Category** | Business / Commercial |
| **Assessment method** | WORK_RECORD_REVIEW |
| **Applies to** | Business Development & External Contracting; SP and above |

## Description

Tracking and managing RFQs, RFPs and ITTs through a structured tender process — making qualify / no‑bid decisions and providing technical and commercial input into winning proposals.

## Proficiency Levels

| Level | Label | What it means for this skill |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Awareness | Understands the tender lifecycle and key document types |
| 2 | Basic | Maintains the tender tracker and assembles bid documents with guidance |
| 3 | Intermediate | Coordinates a bid response end‑to‑end and meets submission compliance |
| 4 | Advanced | Leads bids; drives qualify/no‑bid calls and win strategy with commercial input |
| 5 | Expert | Owns the bid process and win‑rate improvement; mentors bid teams |

## Assessment Methods

Configured inline on the skill (**Admin › Skills › Competency Standard › Assessment Methods**) and conforms to **ISO 10667, ISO/IEC 17024, OPITO**.

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Method | Work Record / Case Study Review |
| Evidence validity | 12 months |
| Min. approved records | 2 |
| Frequency | Any time within the year (annual) |
| Audience | All employees assigned the skill |

**Prompt** — Review tender trackers, qualify/no-bid records and submitted bid documents as evidence.

**Behaviourally-anchored criteria** (rated 1–5, weighted to 100):

- Coordinates a compliant bid response end-to-end and meets submission rules. — **50%**
- Drives a sound qualify / no-bid decision and win strategy. — **50%**

## Notes

Closely tied to Contract Negotiation and Proposal & Technical Writing. Evidence: submitted bids, win/loss records, tender trackers.
