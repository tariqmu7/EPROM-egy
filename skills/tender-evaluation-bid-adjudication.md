## Description

Evaluating received bids and tenders against technical, commercial and compliance criteria — running a structured, auditable adjudication to recommend award decisions that achieve best value and meet governance requirements.

## Proficiency Levels

| Level | Label | What it means for this skill |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Awareness | Knows the stages of bid evaluation and basic technical/commercial criteria |
| 2 | Basic | Compiles received bids and checks completeness and compliance with guidance |
| 3 | Intermediate | Scores bids against agreed technical and commercial criteria independently |
| 4 | Advanced | Designs evaluation models and leads adjudication panels to a justified, auditable award recommendation |
| 5 | Expert | Sets evaluation / adjudication policy and governance; assures award integrity across the function |

**Behaviourally-anchored criteria** (rated 1–5, weighted to 100):

- Applies objective, weighted evaluation criteria fairly. — **50%**
- Produces a defensible, well-documented adjudication recommendation. — **50%**
