## Description

Negotiating contracts with clients, vendors and partners to secure favorable commercial terms while meeting client needs and managing risk.

## Proficiency Levels

| Level | Label | What it means for this skill |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Awareness | Understands common contract terms and the company's red lines |
| 2 | Basic | Supports negotiations and prepares term comparisons |
| 3 | Intermediate | Leads routine negotiations within an approved mandate |
| 4 | Advanced | Negotiates complex/high‑value deals, balancing margin, risk and relationship |
| 5 | Expert | Sets negotiation strategy and playbooks; handles the most strategic deals |
