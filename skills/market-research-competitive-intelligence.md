## Description

Gathering and analysing data on industry trends, client needs, and competitor activity to inform business decisions and identify new opportunities in the oil‑and‑gas market.

## Proficiency Levels

| Level | Label | What it means for this skill |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Awareness | Knows what market research is and where company market data lives |
| 2 | Basic | Collects and summarises published market and competitor data with guidance |
| 3 | Intermediate | Runs structured market studies; profiles competitors and segments independently |
| 4 | Advanced | Synthesises intelligence into opportunity assessments that shape BD decisions |
| 5 | Expert | Builds the market‑intelligence framework; forecasts market shifts and advises leadership |
