# Skill — Client Relationship Management

> إدارة علاقات العملاء

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Category** | Business / Commercial |
| **Assessment method** | THREE_SIXTY_EVALUATION |
| **Applies to** | Business Development & External Contracting; SP and above |

## Description

Building and managing long‑term client and partner relationships — account management, networking and partnership development that sustain and grow business.

## Proficiency Levels

| Level | Label | What it means for this skill |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Awareness | Knows key clients and the basics of professional networking |
| 2 | Basic | Maintains client contact records and handles routine follow‑up |
| 3 | Intermediate | Manages assigned accounts and grows them through proactive engagement |
| 4 | Advanced | Owns strategic accounts; builds trusted partnerships at senior levels |
| 5 | Expert | Sets the account‑management approach; opens doors others cannot |

## Assessment Methods

Configured inline on the skill (**Admin › Skills › Competency Standard › Assessment Methods**) and conforms to **360° Multi-Rater, BARS, SHRM BASK**.

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Method | 360° Multi-Rater Evaluation |
| Rater weights | Self 10% / Peer 30% / Manager 60% |
| Assessor (manager rater) | Direct Manager |
| Frequency | Any time within the year (annual) |
| Audience | All employees assigned the skill |

**Behaviourally-anchored criteria** (rated 1–5, weighted to 100):

- Builds and sustains trusted, long-term client / partner relationships. — **40%**
- Manages and grows assigned accounts through proactive engagement. — **35%**
- Represents the company credibly when networking at senior levels. — **25%**

## Notes

Behavioral skill best evidenced via 360° input from clients and peers plus account‑growth records.
